How to manage dozens or hundreds of sites with Platform.sh

Large fleets of sites pose large problems:

  • Provisioning
  • Updating
  • Access controll
  • Support and problem resolution

Provisioning - automate it

Use the Platform.sh API to create instances of your product. This can enable self service or bulk site creation.

Updating - automate it

Maintain your source code separately and push it out to your fleet of site by scripting our API. You decide what the workflow is. Updating into a new branch is supported, as is updating the live product.

Run commands on multiple projects using the CLI or API calls

Access controll

Adding users as admins, developers, readers, or customers to your product can be scripted and integrated with your authentication and authorisation solution.

Support and problem resolution

We've got your back with 24/7 infrastructure support. Your helpdesk team can escalate relevant tickets to us directly via ticket sharing.
